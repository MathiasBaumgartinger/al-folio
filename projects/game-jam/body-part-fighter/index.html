Body Part Fighter

Build creatures out of scrap body parts and fight with them!

As our contribution to the 2020 Global Game Jam with the motto “Repair”, we created a platform fighting with the name “Body Part Fighter”. A mad scientist has found a passion in “repairing” dead bodies of various creatures and letting them fight against each other. It was created in 48 hours and can be downloaded on the official GGJ website.

Repair your fighters and set them into an epic combat against each other.
